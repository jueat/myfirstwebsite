## ðŸŽ¨ Customization Guide

You can easily customize this template by editing index.html:

- *Changing Text*: Locate the text content inside tags like <h1>, <h2>, <p> and replace them with your own.
- *Modifying Colors*: Look for the <style> section in the <head>. You can change hex codes (e.g., #111, #ffcc00) to match your brand colors.
- *Updating Links*: Change the href="#" attributes in the <a> (anchor) tags to point to your actual pages or sections.
